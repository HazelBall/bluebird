Colorado Bluebird Project

The Colorado Bluebird Project is a volunteer run project operating under the guidance of Audubon Society of Greater Denver. It aims to improve the population and health of Bluebirds in Colorado, and to educate the public about Bluebirds.

Volunteers place and keep up with Bluebird nest boxes to track population, fledgings, eggs, and hatches, in order to gather more information about Bluebirds.

Legend High School's birdhouses

In 2018, Legend High School's EDGE Program set out to create birdhouses to help out the Colorado Bluebird Project. We created a total of 13 nest boxes, placed around the school. My job is to track them during the summer, write down what happens, and record it on this website!

You can find information on bluebirds and their birdhouses and birdhouse outcomes and updates.

Want to create your own birdhouses?

Creating your own birdhouse for bluebirds is easy and fun! Just use our instructions and read through our helpful tips to make sure you see bluebirds when you create and put your birdhouse up!
